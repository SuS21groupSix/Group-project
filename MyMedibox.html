WELCOME TO

myMEDIBOX!

In your personal MyMedibox you can add items to your virtual medicine cabinet, check your inventory, reorder products and much more.
Just have a look!


Check inventory

Have a look at your medicine cabinet and get an overview over your items and their information.

Search inventory

Check whether an item exists in your medicine cabinet.

Add items to inventory

Add items to your medicine cabinet by simply scanning the QR- or barcode of your product.

Remove items from inventory

Remove items from your medicine cabinet when you have disposed of them.

Check expiry dates

Check the expiry dates of your products to see when you will have to replace them.

Reorder items

Reorder your items when you need to.
